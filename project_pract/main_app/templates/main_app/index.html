About the Project

This data science initiative aims to transform raw BASC employee data into actionable intelligence. By analyzing various dimensions such as gender, employment status, and campus assignments, we provide a clear, data-driven perspective on the workforce. Our goal is to support strategic planning and foster a deeper understanding of the employee landscape.

We employ various statistical and machine learning techniques to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies within the data. This project is a testament to the power of data to inform and empower an organization.
